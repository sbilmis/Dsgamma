Bc1 -> Bc gamma perturbative result plus conservative G2 envelope
================================================================

This is not the final explicit G2 correction.  It is the conservative
systematic envelope inferred from the same-window heavy-heavy G2/Pert
ratios after including the residue-normalization effect.

  g_pert = 0.03155 [0.02685, 0.03701] GeV^-1
  Gamma_pert = 0.2233 [0.1618, 0.3075] keV
  conservative G2 envelope: median |dg/g|=0.058, max=0.128; median |dGamma/Gamma|=0.115, max=0.257
  Gamma G2 envelope: median +/-0.025 keV, max +/-0.0599 keV

Conclusion:
- G2 is not proven negligible by this screen.
- The quoted perturbative widths should carry this as a systematic envelope
  until the full radiative G2 double-Borel reduction is completed.
