x-dependent three-particle soft photon-DA E1 kernels
====================================================

Scope: sigma_{ab} part of S_Q^G; x kept explicit.

A_T3_x_raw:
((16*I)*Pair[Momentum[k], Momentum[q]])/Pair[Momentum[p], Momentum[q]]

A_T4_x_raw:
((-16*I)*Pair[Momentum[k], Momentum[q]])/Pair[Momentum[p], Momentum[q]]

B_T3_x_raw:
((16*I)*mQ)/(mQ + ms)

B_T4_x_raw:
((-16*I)*mQ)/(mQ + ms)

Ratio check after k.q -> p.q:
B_T3 / [(mQ/(mQ+ms)) A_T3] = 1
B_T4 / [(mQ/(mQ+ms)) A_T4] = 1
