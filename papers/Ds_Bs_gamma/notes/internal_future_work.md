## Bc1 Radiative Transitions

- Keep \(B_{c1}\) out of the present \(D_{s1},B_{s1}\) manuscript.
- Reason: \(B_{c1}\) is a heavy-heavy system, while the present calculation is
  built for heavy-light strange mesons with photon distribution amplitudes and a
  light strange spectator.
- A serious \(B_{c1}\) analysis would need a separate setup: heavy-heavy
  currents, new two-point residues, a different treatment of the photon
  emission mechanism, and likely NRQCD/potential-model or heavy-heavy QCDSR
  validation.
- Useful future question: whether a Bondar--Milstein-like constructive versus
  destructive interference pattern appears in the \(B_{c1}\) axial partners.
